LEAN is a systematic method to improve efficiency and effectiveness by eliminating wastes (Transportation, Inventory, Motion, Intellectual, Wait time, Overprocessing, Overproduction, Defects) within the process to speed up the delivery. The core principle of Lean is to eliminate non-value adding activities of the process.

It is an illustration of the sequential activities (both value-added and non-value added) that an organization undertakes to deliver the customer request or needs. Its essentially a planning tool to optimize results by eliminating waste.
