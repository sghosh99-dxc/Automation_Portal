I & A

Automation and innovation are closely related. Automation triggers innovation by freeing up resources and providing data and insights, while innovation serves as a spark for automation by leading to the development of new technologies and tools. The interplay between automation and innovation helps to shape the future of work and is critical for the success of organizations in today's rapidly changing business environment.
